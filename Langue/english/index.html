WHO WE ARE

AGENCE REBI SERVICES S.A.R.L. is a multidisciplinary service provider under Congolese law operating within the OHADA system. We are always ready to take on challenges to meet the demands of our partners by providing high-quality goods and services with up-to-date equipment, ensuring that we effectively and efficiently meet the needs of our various partners.

We are partnered with several local and international experts with whom we exchange experiences and resources. We remain attentive to market demands and specificities to deliver results that are both effective and efficient, meeting the needs of our various partners.
